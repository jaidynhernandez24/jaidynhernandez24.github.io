JOB EXPERIENCE

Introduction

Rather than pursuing traditional employment, I chose to build my own path by founding and operating my own buiness. I began my buisness at the age of 13 and have developed it through years of work. Through self-employment, I've gained hands-on experience in branding, costumer service, client communication, production, and problem solving. Running a company independantly has required discipline, creativity, and adaptability. From concept development to execution, I've managed every stage of the process, strengthening both my technical skills and my entrepreneurial mindset. Running a buisness has given me a strong foundation to be creative and confident.

My Buisness

In 2020, at 13, I decided I wanted to turn my creative ideas into a buisness. I began making and selling costumes and clothing. I felt frustrated with the rise of fast fashion and overconsumption. I made a commitment to only use thrifted or eco-friendly materials. Each piece was unique and one of a kind. By 2026, I have made over 400 sales and $80,000 in revenue. To this day I still build and grow my buisness, each piece I make allows me to attain a new skill. I have currently been designing gloves for snowboarding. I paint and use ink on them to make them completely custom. Although being in college has made it difficult to work consistently, I still work as much as I can.
